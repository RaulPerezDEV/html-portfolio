My top 3 movies of all-time

Star Wars III: The Revenge of the Sith

Best film soundtrack ever, great history and one of the sadest plot twist of the history. Simply perfect!

Alien

The best horror film. And it aged very well for a 1979 movie.

Avatar

All in this movie is amazing. The special effects, the history, the character... All I can say is 'WOW'.
